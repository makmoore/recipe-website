Soft and Fluffy Cinnamon Rolls


Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 20-25 minutes
Serving Size: 12 rolls

There's nothing better than waking up to the smell of homemade cinnamon rolls fresh from the oven! These rolls are soft, fluffy, and packed with a sweet cinnamon-sugar filling, all topped with a smooth vanilla glaze. Whether you're making them for a weekend brunch or a special occasion, these homemade cinnamon rolls will make everyone come back for more!



Ingredients (Dough):

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast (1 packet)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

Ingredients (Filling):

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

Ingredients (Glaze):

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract


Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Stir until dough forms.
  3. Knead for about 8 minutes until smooth, then let rise in a greased bowl for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out dough into a 14x9-inch rectangle. Spread softened butter over the surface.
  5. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle over buttered dough.
  6. Roll dough tightly and cut into 12 even slices.
  7. Place rolls in a greased baking dish, cover, and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.
  9. Whisk together glaze ingredients and drizzle over warm rolls.
